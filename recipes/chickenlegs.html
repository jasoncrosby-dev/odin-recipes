Air Fryer Chicken Legs

Ingredients

  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 skin-on chicken drumsticks

Steps

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F according to manufacturerâ€™s instructions. Combine garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper in a small bowl.

  2. Place drumsticks on a clean work surface. Sprinkle spice mix evenly over all sides of the drumsticks.

  3. Place drumsticks in the basket of the air fryer and cook for 10 minutes. Using tongs, flip chicken over and cook for 10 minutes more.

  4. Rest for 5 minutes before serving.

An instant-read thermometer inserted near the bone should read 165 degrees F.
